Hello.

We are Déjà Brew. We work hard to present a warm, pleasing place that is more in keeping with a traditional coffee house—a place for the exchange of ideas and a meeting place for the community. Along with our commitment to the arts, we feel strongly about creating a great cup of coffee, in every single cup. As an independent business we fulfill a niche in the community to combine these three elements—atmosphere, art, and high-quality coffee in a unique way. We hope we have succeeded.
